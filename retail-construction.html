Common Questions

Retail Build FAQs

Can you fit out a shop while the shopping centre is open?

Yes. We regularly work within occupied shopping centres and retail parks. We manage noise, dust, and deliveries to comply with landlord requirements and minimise disruption to neighbouring tenants.

Do you do multi-site retail rollouts?

Absolutely. We can replicate your brand concept across multiple locations with consistent quality. We develop standardised specifications and can manage simultaneous fits across the North West.

How quickly can you fit out a retail unit?

A standard high-street shop fit-out takes 4 to 8 weeks. Larger supermarkets and showrooms take 10 to 16 weeks. We always work backwards from your target opening date and can fast-track if needed.

Do you handle landlord licence to alter applications?

Yes. We prepare and submit Licence to Alter applications on your behalf, including technical drawings and specifications that satisfy landlord requirements for structural or M&E changes.
