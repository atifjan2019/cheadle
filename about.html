Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2003

Cheadle Construction was founded with a simple vision: to deliver construction projects of the highest quality, built with integrity, craftsmanship, and a genuine care for our clients. Over the past two decades, that vision has guided every project we've undertaken.

From our beginnings as a small team of passionate tradespeople in Cheadle, Stockport, we have grown into one of the most trusted and respected construction companies in the North West. Our portfolio now spans luxury residential builds, large-scale commercial projects, and everything in between.

How We Work

A Proven Process for Outstanding Results

Every successful project begins with a clear plan. Our approach has been refined over 20 years of experience to ensure your project runs smoothly from the very first conversation to the final handover.

01

Free Consultation & Site Survey

We visit your property, discuss your vision, assess the site, and provide expert advice — all completely free of charge with no obligation.

02

Design & Planning

Our team works with architects and planners to create detailed designs, handle planning applications, and prepare building regulation submissions.

03

Build & Construction

With your approval, our experienced team begins construction. We assign a dedicated project manager who keeps you informed with regular updates.

04

Completion & Aftercare

On completion, we conduct a full snagging inspection with you. Every project includes a 10-year structural warranty and 2-year defects liability period.
