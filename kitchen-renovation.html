Worktop Materials

Choosing the Right Worktop

The worktop is the centrepiece of your kitchen. Here are the most popular options we install.

Quartz

Engineered stone with consistent colour and pattern. Extremely hard-wearing, non-porous, and low maintenance. The most popular premium choice.

From £80/m²

Granite

Natural stone with unique veining. Heat resistant, incredibly durable, and each slab is one-of-a-kind. A classic high-end option.

From £100/m²

Dekton / Ceramic

Ultra-compact surfaces resistant to heat, scratches, UV, and stains. Ultra-thin profiles available for a sleek, modern aesthetic.

From £120/m²

Common Questions

Kitchen Renovation FAQs

How long does a kitchen renovation take?

A small to medium kitchen takes 2-3 weeks. A large kitchen with island and structural work takes 3-6 weeks. We provide a detailed programme so you can plan around the disruption.

Do you supply the kitchen units or can I source my own?

Both. We work with quality kitchen suppliers, but we're equally happy to install a kitchen you've selected yourself — from Howdens, Wren, or any other supplier. We charge the same for installation either way.

Can you remove a wall to create an open-plan kitchen?

Yes — this is one of our most requested kitchen renovations. We handle the structural calculations, steel beam installation, and building control approval as part of the project.

What about gas and electrics?

All our gas work is completed by Gas Safe registered engineers and all electrical work is certified to Part P regulations. You'll receive all necessary certificates on completion.
