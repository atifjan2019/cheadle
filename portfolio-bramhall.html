Project Overview

From Dated to Stunning

This substantial project involved multiple extensions on a dated property in Bramhall, transforming it into a stylish, modern family home. The full reconfiguration of rooms allowed us to reimagine the layout from the ground up.

The scope included a new kitchen, fully redesigned bathrooms, and a showcase master suite, all finished to the highest standard with premium materials and meticulous attention to detail.
