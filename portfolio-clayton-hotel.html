Commercial Excellence

While 95% of our work is domestic, we selectively take on commercial projects where we can deliver real value. The Clayton Hotel Manchester engaged us for roof repairs and internal fit-out works — a project that demanded precision, reliability, and minimal disruption to hotel operations.

Our team worked closely with hotel management to schedule works around guest occupancy, ensuring the highest standards were maintained throughout the project.
