Common Questions

Garden & Landscaping FAQs

Do I need planning permission for a patio?

Generally no, patios don't require planning permission as long as they don't significantly raise the level of your garden. However, if your property is listed or in a conservation area, restrictions may apply. Driveways may need permeable surfaces under planning rules.

How long does a patio or driveway take?

A typical patio takes 3 to 5 days. A standard driveway takes 5 to 10 days. Larger landscaping projects with walls, steps, and multiple areas take 2 to 4 weeks. Weather can affect timescales.

Can you work in winter?

Yes, though some tasks (like pointing and rendering) are weather-dependent. We plan projects to avoid frost-sensitive work during the coldest months. Groundwork, drainage, and excavation can proceed year-round.

Do you handle the waste removal?

Absolutely. All excavated materials, old paving, concrete, and green waste are removed from site. We use licensed waste carriers and recycle wherever possible.
