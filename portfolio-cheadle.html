Project Overview

Timeless Modern Traditional Design

This ongoing project in a Cheadle conservation area combines a loft conversion with a rear ground floor extension, new kitchen, and new bathrooms — all designed with a timeless modern traditional aesthetic.

Working within a conservation area requires careful planning and consideration of local regulations. Our design approach ensures the property is future-proofed for family living while respecting the character of the surrounding area.
