Our Accreditations

Professional Certifications

Our accreditations are your guarantee of quality, safety, and professionalism.

Federation of Master Builders

Membership of the FMB is independently assessed and guarantees that we meet rigorous quality standards. All FMB members are vetted, financially checked, and regularly inspected.

📄 View FMB Certificate 📄 Our FMB Membership

TrustMark Registered

TrustMark is a government-endorsed quality scheme covering work carried out in and around the home. Our registration demonstrates our commitment to good customer service and technical competence.

CHAS Certificate

CHAS Accredited

The Contractors Health & Safety Assessment Scheme certifies that our health and safety management systems meet the required standards set by the UK government.

CSCS Certificate

CSCS Certified Operatives

All our tradespeople hold valid Construction Skills Certification Scheme cards, proving their competence, qualifications, and health & safety awareness.

CDM Certificate

CDM 2015 Compliant

We fully comply with the Construction (Design and Management) Regulations 2015 on every project — ensuring safety is designed into every build from the outset.

Building Regs Certificate

Building Regulations

Every project is designed and built to meet or exceed current Building Regulations. All work receives full building control sign-off upon completion.
