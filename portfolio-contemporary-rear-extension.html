Project Overview

This contemporary rear extension was designed to create a stunning open-plan kitchen, dining, and living space for a growing family in Wilmslow. The brief was to maximise natural light and create a seamless connection with the garden.

The project involved removing the existing rear wall and replacing it with a large structural steel beam to create a wide open-plan layout. Full-height sliding doors were installed to flood the space with light and open directly onto the landscaped patio.

Key Features

  • 5.5m × 8m single storey rear extension
  • Full-height aluminium sliding doors
  • Bespoke kitchen with quartz worktops
  • Underfloor heating throughout
  • Vaulted ceiling with roof lantern
  • Integrated LED lighting scheme
  • Matching patio and landscaping
