Understanding Prestbury

Village Character

Prestbury's architecture spans centuries — and each style requires a different approach.

🏡

Stone Cottages

Original village cottages in local sandstone and gritstone. We repair, restore, and sensitively extend these characterful properties.

🏛️

Georgian & Victorian

Larger period homes with original fireplaces, cornicing, and sash windows. Internal modernisation whilst preserving key features.

🏰

Country Houses

Substantial properties on large plots. Major renovations, wing additions, orangeries, and outbuilding conversions.

🏠

Modern Executive Homes

Contemporary properties built this century. Extensions, interior upgrades, and garden room additions to enhance family living.

Heritage Craftsmanship

Traditional Building Skills

Prestbury's older properties demand building techniques that most modern builders simply don't possess. Our team includes craftsmen with genuine expertise in traditional methods:

✓ Natural hydraulic lime mortar pointing
✓ Stone masonry and piecing-in repairs
✓ Sash window refurbishment and draught-proofing
✓ Traditional oak framing for extensions
✓ Breathable insulation for solid walls
✓ Reclaimed materials sourcing and feature reuse
Planning Guidance

Planning in Prestbury

📋

Conservation Area Rules

Prestbury's conservation area restricts permitted development. Most alterations need full planning permission — we handle the entire application.

🌿

Green Belt Sensitivity

Surrounding green belt limits extension sizes and new development. We design within these constraints to maximise what's achievable.

📐

Pre-Application Strategy

We recommend pre-application consultations with Cheshire East to test proposals before committing to a full application and associated fees.
