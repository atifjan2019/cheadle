Common Questions

Bathroom FAQs

How long does a bathroom renovation take?

A standard bathroom renovation takes 1-2 weeks. More complex projects involving layout changes, underfloor heating, or wet rooms may take 2-3 weeks. We always complete one bathroom before starting another.

Can I use the bathroom during renovation?

Unfortunately not — the plumbing will be disconnected during the renovation. If you have a second bathroom, you can use that. If not, we plan the work to minimise the time your bathroom is out of use.

Do you supply the sanitaryware?

We can supply everything, or you can purchase your own suite, tiles, and fixtures. We're happy to advise on good quality suppliers and accompany you to showrooms if helpful.

Do I need waterproofing?

Yes, all shower and wet areas are fully tanked (waterproofed) prior to tiling. This prevents water damage to the structure and is essential for a long-lasting bathroom installation.
