Seasonal Guide

When to Maintain Your Home

Preventative maintenance throughout the year saves money and protects your property.

🌸 Spring

Inspect for winter damage. Check roof tiles, clean gutters, clear drains, and repair any frost damage to pointing and rendering.

☀ Summer

Best time for exterior painting and rendering. Repair cracks, repoint brickwork, and treat timber windows and doors.

🍂 Autumn

Clear leaves from gutters and drains. Check flashings and seals. Prepare for winter with roof and chimney inspections.

❄ Winter

Emergency repairs only. Monitor for leaks, ice damage, and blocked drainage. Plan spring maintenance projects.

Warning Signs

Signs Your Property Needs Attention

Don't wait until small problems become structural damage. Watch for these common warning signs:

⚠ Cracked or missing pointing between bricks
⚠ Water stains on internal walls or ceilings
⚠ Blocked, leaking, or overflowing gutters
⚠ Slipped, cracked, or missing roof tiles
⚠ Peeling paint or blown render on external walls
⚠ Rotten or soft timber on window frames or fascias
Common Questions

Exterior Maintenance FAQs

Do you do small exterior repairs or only large jobs?

We do both. Whether it's replacing a few slipped tiles, repointing a small section of brickwork, or a complete exterior refurbishment — no job is too small or too large.

Can you work on listed or conservation properties?

Yes. We have experience maintaining listed buildings and properties in conservation areas. We use appropriate materials (lime mortar, traditional render, heritage-appropriate paints) and can liaise with conservation officers.

Is scaffolding always needed?

Not always. Many jobs can be done from ladders or cherry pickers. If scaffolding is needed, it's included in our quotation and we arrange everything including council footpath licences if required.

How often should I have my home's exterior checked?

We recommend an annual exterior inspection, ideally in spring after winter weather. This identifies small issues before they become expensive problems. We offer inspection reports as part of our service.
