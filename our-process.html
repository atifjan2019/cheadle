The 6 Steps

Your Project Journey

Every project follows our proven 6-step process, ensuring quality at every stage and peace of mind for you.

01

Initial Consultation

We visit your property for a free site survey and discussion. We listen to your ideas, assess the site, discuss budgets, and provide expert advice — all with absolutely no obligation.

  • Free site visit
  • Budget discussion
  • Feasibility assessment
  • Expert advice
02

Design & Planning

Our team works alongside architects and planners to create detailed designs. We manage planning applications and building regulation submissions on your behalf to save you time and stress.

  • Architectural drawings
  • Planning applications
  • Building regulations
  • Party wall agreements
03

Quotation & Agreement

We provide a clear, itemised quotation with no hidden extras. Once you're happy, we agree a fixed-price contract and set a detailed programme of works with confirmed milestones.

  • Detailed itemised quote
  • Fixed-price contracts
  • Clear payment schedule
  • Project timeline
04

Construction Phase

Our experienced tradesmen carry out all work to the highest standards. Your dedicated project manager provides regular progress updates and is always available to answer questions.

  • Skilled tradespeople
  • Regular site updates
  • Quality inspections
  • H&S compliance
05

Quality Inspection

Before handover, every project undergoes a thorough snagging inspection. We walk through every detail with you to ensure everything meets our exacting standards and your complete satisfaction.

  • Full snagging list
  • Client walkthrough
  • Building control sign-off
  • Final clean
06

Handover & Aftercare

We complete a full handover with all documentation, warranties, and certificates. Our aftercare service means we're always here if you need us — backed by a 10-year structural warranty.

  • Full documentation pack
  • 10-year warranty
  • 2-year defects period
  • Ongoing aftercare

Common Questions

Process FAQs

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope. A single-storey extension typically takes 8–12 weeks, while a full new build can take 6–9 months. We always provide a detailed timeline before work begins.

Do you handle planning permission?

Yes, we manage the entire planning and building regulation process on your behalf. Our team has extensive experience working with local planning authorities across Stockport, Manchester, and Cheshire.

What if I want to make changes during the build?

We understand that ideas evolve. Any changes are discussed with your project manager, priced transparently, and only proceed with your written approval. We never make changes without your consent.

How do payments work?

Payments are staged and tied to agreed milestones in the programme. You only pay for completed work. All payment terms are clearly set out in our contract before work begins.
