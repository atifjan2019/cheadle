Sensitive Building in Bowdon

Bowdon is one of Trafford's most affluent areas, known for its leafy streets, substantial period properties, and protected conservation area. Building here requires sensitivity, planning expertise, and an understanding of what Trafford Council expects.

We have extensive experience navigating Bowdon's conservation requirements — ensuring your project enhances both your property and the wider streetscape.

Planning Expertise

Bowdon Conservation Area Planning

Many Bowdon properties sit within the conservation area, where permitted development rights are restricted.

📋

Design Statements

Preparing heritage and design statements that demonstrate how your project respects and enhances the conservation area character.

🏛

Material Selection

Sourcing reclaimed brick, natural stone, and roofing materials that match the existing streetscape and satisfy planning conditions.

📐

Sensitive Scale

Designing extensions that are proportionate and subordinate to the original building — a key requirement in conservation areas.

🌳

Tree Protection

Managing construction around protected trees with root protection zones, arboricultural supervision, and tree officer liaison.
